About MageStone

MageStone is an original fantasy strategy board game — "magical chess with dice" — currently in open alpha. The browser version you can play here is a full 3D digital prototype of a game designed for the tabletop: a 16×16 board in a candlelit stone chamber, painted miniatures, and real physics dice.

Every rule you play online is the tabletop rule. The digital alpha exists to playtest the design at scale before the physical edition: every game, win and piece of feedback shapes the balance of the final game.

Interested in the physical game?

A print-and-play kit is planned. Join the playtest list from the Print & Play link in the game's footer, and you'll be first to hear when it's ready.

Feedback

The alpha has a built-in feedback form (the Feedback button in the game and on the front page). Everything submitted lands directly with the designer — bug reports, balance gripes and ideas are all welcome.
